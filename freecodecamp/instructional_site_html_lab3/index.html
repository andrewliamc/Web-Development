Learning A Little Guitar With Three Songs

Ready to play guitar but don't know where to start? We've got you covered! With just a few simple chords, you'll learn to play three classic songs that sound great and build your confidence. No complicated theory—just hands-on, easy-to-follow lessons that get you strumming right away. Grab your guitar and start playing today!

Beginner Level: 'Stand by Me' by Ben E. King

This tutorial teaches the classic song “Stand by Me,” using simple chords and a straightforward strumming pattern, making it perfect for beginners.

Intermediate Level: 'Hotel California' by The Eagles

This lesson breaks down the iconic song “Hotel California,” covering its intricate chord progressions and strumming patterns suitable for intermediate players.

Advanced Level: 'Cliffs of Dover' by Eric Johnson

This tutorial delves into the advanced techniques required to play “Cliffs of Dover,” focusing on its complex riffs and speed.
